what's new

  • School Canteen

    The school canteen is hygenic and clean. It serves only vegetarian food throughout the school time and in day care. Menu is on rotational basis. Students can also opt for monthly lunch from canteen. Apart from this, school canteen also serves snacks on paid basis. In the menu they have several item like:-



    Rajma Rice

    Kadhi Rice

    Chole Rice

    Pasta

    Sandwich

    Veg Cutlets

    And also serves juices on paid basis and much more!

  • Implemented School App

    The school app consists of:-



    News: This will tell you about everything your child has learned in school.

    Notice: This will inform you about all the informations and notices.

    Homework: This will tell you if your child is assigned with any homework or not.

    Online Class: This will tell you if there is a holiday and on what days do you have your online classes.

    E-Content: This will tell you that will there be any upcoming Curricular, co-Curricular or Extracurricular activities.

    Lesson Plan: This will tell you what lessons will your child learn in the upcoming classes.

    Attendance: This will tell you for how many days was your child absent or, if your child is regular.

    Fees: This will tell you how much fee do you have to give.

    Diary: You can chat with the school manager or director.

    Syllabus: This will tell you what is your upcoming syllabus.

    Exam: This will tell you when is your upcoming exam.

    Calender: Works like a normal Calender
